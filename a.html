Shipping Policy

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery within 3-5 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Estimated delivery within 1-2 business days.
  • Same Day Delivery: Available for select locations. Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM local time.

Shipping Rates

Our shipping rates are calculated based on the total weight of your order and the destination address. The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process.

Order Processing Time

Orders are usually processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information.

International Shipping

Currently, we only offer shipping within the United States. We do not ship internationally.

Shipping FAQs

1. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your order on our website.

2. What if my order is delayed?

In rare cases, there may be delays in shipping due to unforeseen circumstances such as extreme weather conditions or carrier delays. If your order is delayed, we will notify you via email and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

3. What if I'm not available to receive my order?

If you're not available to receive your order, the carrier will usually leave a delivery attempt notice with instructions for rescheduling or picking up your order from a local facility.

4. Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Once an order has been placed, the shipping address cannot be changed. Please make sure to review your shipping address carefully before completing the checkout process.
